About me

Hi, my name is Gosia. I was born in a small town in the south of Poland called Rybnik. Right now I am trying to redirect my life and become a front-end developer - a profession where I can see the results of my work, often almost immediately. That is why I chose to start programming with YouCode. I am determined to learn the ins and outs of HTML, CSS and the JavaScript language, to be able to create great, accessible websites.

Recently

From 2012 till 2019 I studied Law at the Adam Mickiewicz University in Poznan. Having obtained my Master's degree, I worked as an insurance claims handler in Warsaw for a year. After moving to Switzerland 🧀 to live with my boyfriend, I decided that I wanted to change career paths and decided to try out front-end development.
